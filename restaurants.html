Dining on the Island

From local fresh-caught fish to 24-hour convenience, Taniti offers a diverse culinary experience for every traveler.

Local Flavors

Enjoy local fish and rice at one of our ten authentic island restaurants.

International Variety

Explore American-style burgers or high-end pan-Asian cuisine located in Taniti City.

24-Hour Groceries

Two supermarkets are available for those who prefer fresh ingredients and self-catering.

Important Information
