Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know for a smooth 7-day paradise stay in Taniti.

What currency is used in Taniti?

The local currency is the U.S. Dollar (USD). While some local businesses may accept other currencies, USD is the standard.

Are credit cards accepted?

Yes, major credit cards are widely accepted across the island.

What is the electrical voltage?

Power outlets are 120 volts, which is the standard for the United States.

What are the public transportation hours?

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily.

Is Taniti safe for tourists?

Taniti is very safe; violent crime is rare. However, we always recommend standard travel precautions.

Are there 24-hour food options?

Yes, there are two major supermarkets on the island that are open 24 hours for your convenience.
