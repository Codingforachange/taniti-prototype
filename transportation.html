Getting Around Taniti

From public transit to private rentals, we have options to help you explore every corner of the island.

Public Transportation

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily. Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxis & Rentals

Taxis are available in Taniti City. You can also rent cars, bicycles, and helmets near the airport.

Walkability

Taniti City is very walkable, allowing you to enjoy the local scenery and shops at your own pace.
