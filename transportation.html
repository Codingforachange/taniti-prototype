Getting Around Taniti

Public Transportation

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily.

Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxis & Rentals

Taxis are available in Taniti City.

Rental cars, bikes, and helmets can be rented near the airport.

Taniti City is very walkable.

Essential Information

Currency

The local currency is the U.S. Dollar (USD). Major credit cards are accepted.

Power Outlets

Voltage is 120V (Standard US outlets).

Health & Safety

A hospital and several clinics are available. Most staff are multilingual.
